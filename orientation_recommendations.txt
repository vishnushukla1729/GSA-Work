
Recommendations based on the analysis:
1. Enhance Workshop Rotations: Review and improve the content of Workshop Rotations 2 and 3 for transfer students to increase satisfaction levels.
2. Focus on Health and Wellness: Allocate more time during orientation to discuss campus health and wellness resources, particularly Student Health Services and The Well Services and Events.
